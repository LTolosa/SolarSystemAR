Venus is in retrograde rotation. This means that Venus rotates in the opposite direction than other planets.
==
Venus is the second brightest object you can see in the night sky.
==
It takes Venus 243 Earth days to rotate on its axis. Its orbit takes 225 Earth days. So a day is longer than a year!
==
The average surface tempurature is 462 degrees Celcius. It is the hottest planet in the Solar System.
