Mars' gets its red color from iron oxide, also known as rust, on the surface.
==
Mars' atmostphere is too thin for water to exist in its liquid form, it can only exist as a vapor or ice.
==
Large and violent dust storms are frequent on the surface of Mars. They have winds up to 125 mph and can last for weeks.
==
It takes Mars 687 days to orbit the sun.
==
The gravity on Mars is 37.5% of the gravity that Earth has. So a 100 pound person would weight only 38 pounds!
==
On Mars the average temperature is -81 degrees Fahreneit, it ranges from -205 degrees in the winter to 72 degrees in the summer.
