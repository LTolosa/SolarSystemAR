Uranus spins in retrograde rotation, like Venus. It takes about 17 hours and 14 minutes to complete a full rotation.
==
It takes Uranus 84 Earth years to orbit around the Sun.
==
Uranus hits the coldest temperatures of any planet in the Solar System, going as low as -224 degrees Celcius.
==
Uranus's rings are tilted on their side so they go up and down!
