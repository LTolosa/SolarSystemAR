Jupiter is fourth brightest object you can see in the night sky.
==
Jupiter has the shortest day of all the planets. One day on Jupiter is 9 hours and 55 minutes.
==
Jupiter makes one complete orbit around the sun every 11.8 Earth years.
==
The giant red spot is a huge storm that has lasted for more that 350 years. It is big enough to fit 3 Earths.
==
Like all other gas giants, Jupiter has a ring but it is very thin.
